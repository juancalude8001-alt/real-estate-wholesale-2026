# Approval Workflow - Quick Start Guide

**5-Minute Overview of Human-Controlled Deal System**

---

## 🎯 The Core Concept

```
AI suggests 3 offers → HUMAN approves 1 → System sends approved offer
```

That's it. Human makes the decision. AI just makes it fast and organized.

---

## ⚡ Quick Steps

### 1️⃣ Generate Offers (Automated)

Property comes in from pipeline. System automatically generates 3 offers:

| Tier | Price | Accept Rate | Your Profit |
|------|-------|-------------|------------|
| 🔴 Aggressive | Lowest | 30% | Highest |
| 🟡 Balanced | Medium | 60% | High |
| 🟢 Conservative | Highest | 80% | Good |

**Example:** MAO $86,500 → Offers: $82,175 / $86,500 / $90,825

### 2️⃣ You Review Dashboard

Dashboard shows:
- Seller name & motivation level
- Three offer options (side-by-side)
- AI recommendation  
- Profit potential for each

### 3️⃣ You Click "Approve" 👆

Click on the tier you like. Done.

System logs:
- Who approved (you)
- When you approved (timestamp)
- Which tier you chose
- For audit trail

### 4️⃣ System Sends Offer (Automated)

SMS to seller: "Hi [Name], I have cash offer $X for your property..."

Email with formal offer letter.

### 5️⃣ Wait for Response

Seller calls back with:
- ✅ **Accepted** → Generate contract
- 📞 **Counter-offer** → Negotiate or walk
- ❌ **Rejected** → Try next tier or dead lead
- 🤐 **No response** → Follow up tomorrow

### 6️⃣ Contract Generation (When Accepted)

Click "Generate Contract"

System creates professional purchase agreement with:
- Property details
- Price & terms
- AS-IS condition
- **7-day exit clause** (your escape route)
- Assignment rights (your profit protection)
- Seller & buyer signature lines

### 7️⃣ Get Signature & Close

Send contract to seller.  
Seller signs.  
Earnest money deposited.  
Title search initiated.  
Close in 14 days.  
Profit realized. ✅

---

## 🚨 Critical Gates (Where You Must Approve)

### Gate 1: Which Offer Tier?
- AI recommends, **you choose**
- Can pick any of the 3 tiers
- Decision is logged for audit

### Gate 2: Generate Contract?
- Only after seller accepts
- **You confirm** before contract is created
- Prevents sending too early

### Gate 3: Send to Seller?
- After contract generated
- **You review** before sending
- Can edit message if needed

## 📋 Approval Checklist

Before clicking "Approve":

- [ ] **MAO Check** - Is offer within MAO limits? (System checks this)
- [ ] **Market Sense** - Does price make sense for market? (You decide)
- [ ] **Seller Motivation** - Matches tier selection? (Check Vapi notes)
- [ ] **Timeline** - 14-day close realistic? (Usually yes)
- [ ] **Profit** - Is profit worth the effort? (Usually yes)

## 🎯 Decision Tree

**What do I do when...?**

### Seller Doesn't Respond
→ Wait 24 hours → Send SMS reminder → Call if needed

### Seller Counter-Offers
→ Can we meet in middle? → Yes: negotiate → No: send next tier

### Seller Wants to List
→ "Keep my offer as backup. I'll be here." → Move on

### Seller Wants More Time  
→ "Offer valid 7 days. Let me know." → Follow up day 6

### Seller Accepts
→ Generate contract immediately → Send for signature

### Multiple Offers on Table
→ Pick one, send → If rejected, try next tier → Done

## 📞 When to Call Seller

**Call if:**
- ✅ Offer accepted (confirm & excite them)
- ✅ Counter-offer received (negotiate)
- ✅ 48 hours, no response (check in)
- ✅ Contract ready (explain terms)

**Don't call:**
- ❌ Right after offer sent (let them read)
- ❌ Before 9 AM (respect)
- ❌ After 9 PM (respect)
- ❌ More than 1x daily (too pushy)

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Start with Balanced
```
Most deals should start with balanced offer.
Aggressive only if high motivation confirmed.
Conservative only if you really need it.
```

### Tip 2: Use SMS + Email
```
SMS: Gets their attention
Email: Gives them details
Both: Maximum response rate
```

### Tip 3: Call After Email
```
Send offer via SMS + email
Wait 2 hours
Call to confirm they got it: "Did you see the email?"
This works better than just hoping
```

### Tip 4: Emphasize Speed
```
"We close in 14 days with cash."
This is your biggest advantage.
Lead with it.
```

### Tip 5: Be Fair
```
Never insult the seller with low-ball offer.
Balanced tier usually hits this sweet spot.
Aggressive only if they earned it with urgency.
```
